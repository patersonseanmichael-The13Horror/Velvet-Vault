Velvet Vault

Terms of Entry

Velvet Vault is a slots-only members product. Account access, wallet requests, and gameplay outcomes are governed by the live rules below and the authoritative Firebase wallet layer.

Account Use

  • You are responsible for maintaining control of your login credentials and UID.
  • Access may be suspended if account details, payout details, or payment proof appear inconsistent or fraudulent.
  • Members must provide accurate profile information during signup.

Wallet Rules

  • Cash balance changes only through deposits, withdrawal approvals, reserve/debit, settle/credit, or cancellation flows.
  • Bonus balance and rollover rules are enforced separately from cash balance.
  • Pending requests remain subject to manual review.

Slots Only

  • Velvet Vault currently offers slots only.
  • No blackjack, poker, or roulette gameplay is part of the active product.
  • Machine access flows through the Slots Lobby and current game pages only.

Member Conduct

  • Do not abuse payments, withdrawals, account creation, or spin replay flows.
  • Admin and monitoring systems log misuse, duplicate requests, and abuse attempts.
  • Use the responsible play resources if play stops feeling controlled.
