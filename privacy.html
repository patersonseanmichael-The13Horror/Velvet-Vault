Privacy

How Member Data Is Handled

Velvet Vault keeps profile, wallet, request, and security data only for operating the members area, enforcing controls, and reviewing payments or withdrawals.

Profile Data

Name, email, phone, date of birth, and UID are stored to run authentication, member support, and payout verification.

Wallet Records

Ledger entries, request statuses, and rollover state are stored for account history, auditability, and dispute handling.

Operational Logs

System logs and spin session records may capture abuse attempts, failed requests, and audit metadata for security and support.
